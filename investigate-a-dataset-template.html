Introduction¶

For this project, I have selected a dataset which collects information from 100k medical appointments in Brazil and focused on the question of whether or not patients will show up for their appointment. Also each row has a unique charactaristic about the patient. I have placed a question "What factors are important for us to know in order to predict if a patient will show up for their scheduled appointment?". Throughout the analysis process, I found that most of patients charactaristics affects their attendance.

Conclusions¶

From the analysis above, we found that if a patient has a chronic disease like Diabetes and Hipertension his Probability of showing up to the appointment will rise. But the Gender doesn't affect the showing of patients since 80% of both males and females showed. Also if patients have Scholarships they will be eager to show. And we noticed that most of Handicap patients show up to the appointment. While I am analysis I thought that a few of alchololisms will show to their appointment but the results contaduct my expectation since we found that most patients with alchoholism showed to their appointment. Lastly, we studied if the age affects the attendance of patients and we found that old people (55 years old and older) mostly show to the appointment more that other ages!

Finally, We can predect that if a patients have some of the above charactaristic his probability of showing to the appointment is higher than others.

In my openion, if the dataset has informations about the distance between the hospital and where the patient lives it will be easer to predect that the patients who will show, the hospital is in or near to their hometown because sometimes it is hard for patients to travel and attend their appointments on time.
